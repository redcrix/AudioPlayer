About App

(M'dzina la Allah Wachifundo Chambiri, Wachisoni Chosatha)

Indeed, all praise is due to Allah and may His peace and blessings be upon His last and final messenger Muhammad (ﷺ).

This mobile app has been put together to serve as a reminder to the Muslim brothers and sisters around the world in particular those who speak the Chichewa language. The English translations have been taken from Sahih International and the Chichewa translations are from the common Chichewa translated Qur'an by Sheikh Khalid Ibrahim (Malawi).

Please feel free to share with others and contact me if you have any suggestions or thoughts on the material.
